Care & Maintenance

Caring for Your Suits

Different fabrics require different levels of care. Follow these guidelines to keep your suits pristine.

Download Care Guide
🫧

Dry Clean Only

All embroidered and silk suits must be dry-cleaned. Machine washing damages embroidery threads and delicate fabrics.

🌡️

Low-Heat Iron

Iron on low heat with a pressing cloth between the iron and fabric. Never iron directly on embroidery.

🌤️

Dry in Shade

If hand-washing lawn suits, dry flat in shade to prevent color fading and fabric distortion.

📦

Hang or Fold Carefully

Hang formal suits on padded hangers. Fold lawn casually. Avoid cramming into tight spaces.
