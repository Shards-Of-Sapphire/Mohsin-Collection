Care & Maintenance

Caring for Gold Plated Jewelry

Gold plating is a delicate finish that rewards simple care habits. Follow these tips to keep the golden glow lasting.

Download Care Guide
💧

Avoid Water & Sweat

Always remove before bathing, swimming, or intense exercise. Moisture accelerates tarnishing.

🧴

Last On, First Off

Wear jewelry after applying makeup, perfume, and hairspray. Remove before skincare routines.

🪶

Gentle Wipe

Use a dry microfibre cloth after wearing. Never use silver/gold polish — it strips the plating.

📦

Anti-tarnish Storage

Store in anti-tarnish pouches provided, away from humidity and direct sunlight.
